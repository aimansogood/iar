Mechanical Parts

Grass cutters are an equipment designed to ease the process of cutting grass. There are various types of grass cutters available such as lawn mowers and robot grass cutters. One of the cheaper options available and most commonly used is the backpack grass cutter. The grass cutters available in market are mostly gasoline powered.

Electrical Parts

To cut grass around a tree, backpack grass cutters with blades moving in circular motion can be used however blades that move back and forth can also be used since it will be able to cut the grass in one revolution only. When compared to conventional grass cutter, the performance of grass cutter with blades that move back and forth is still unknown and will be explored throughout this project.
