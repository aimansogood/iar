    Introduction

    Grass cutters are an equipment designed to ease the process of cutting grass. There are various types of grass cutters available such as lawn mowers and robot grass cutters. One of the cheaper options available and most commonly used is the backpack grass cutter. The grass cutters available in market are mostly gasoline powered.

    Problem Statement

    To cut grass around a tree, backpack grass cutters with blades moving in circular motion can be used however blades that move back and forth can also be used since it will be able to cut the grass in one revolution only. When compared to conventional grass cutter, the performance of grass cutter with blades that move back and forth is still unknown and will be explored throughout this project.

    Our Solution

    Discover how our meticulously designed solution harnesses cutting-edge technology to revolutionize the way you maintain your lawn.

    Efficient cutting with hedge trimmer-like blades
    Our grass cutter employs blades similar to hedge trimmers, enabling it to cut a larger area of grass in each revolution. This design maximizes cutting efficiency, significantly reducing the time and effort required for grass maintenance.
    Custom railing for precise navigation around trees
    We have developed a custom-made railing system specifically designed to guide the movement of the grass cutter around trees. This innovative feature ensures precise and controlled navigation, allowing the grass cutter to maneuver smoothly around obstacles like tree trunks. Achieve even and precise grass cutting, even in challenging areas.
    Solar charge controller for optimized battery charging
    Equipped with a solar charge controller, our grass cutter efficiently charges its battery using solar power. This advanced controller effectively regulates voltage and current from the solar panels, optimizing battery charging and maximizing battery life. Minimize the need for external power sources while benefiting from long-lasting performance.
